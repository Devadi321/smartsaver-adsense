10 Easy Ways to Save $500 This Month

By SmartSaver Team | February 2026 | 8 min read

Want to save $500 this month? It's easier than you think! Here are 10 practical strategies that can add up to big savings without feeling deprived.

1. Cancel Unused Subscriptions

Review your bank statements for recurring charges. Netflix, Spotify, gym memberships - they add up fast. Cancel anything you haven't used in the last month.

Potential savings: $20-100/month

2. Meal Prep Like a Pro

Cooking at home is significantly cheaper than eating out. Dedicate Sunday to meal prep. Make large batches of breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Potential savings: $200-400/month

3. Switch to Generic Brands

Store brands are often made by the same companies as name brands. Switch to generic for groceries, medications, and household items.

Potential savings: $50-100/month

4. Use Cashback Apps

Apps like Rakuten, Ibotta, and Fetch Rewards give you money back on purchases you're already making.

Potential savings: $20-50/month

5. Negotiate Your Bills

Call your internet, insurance, and phone providers. Ask for promotions or better rates. Companies often have retention discounts they don't advertise.

Potential savings: $50-150/month

6. Challenge Yourself to a No-Spend Weekend

Pick one weekend where you don't spend any money. No dining out, no shopping, no entertainment costs.

Potential savings: $50-100/weekend

7. Use Public Transportation

If possible, ditch the car for public transit. You'll save on gas, parking, and maintenance.

Potential savings: $100-300/month

8. Shop with a List

Never go to the store without a list. Stick to exactly what you need to avoid impulse purchases.

Potential savings: $50-100/month

9. Cut the Cord

Cancel cable and switch to streaming services. Or go even simpler with an antenna for local channels.

Potential savings: $50-100/month

10. Review Insurance

Shop around for better rates on auto, home, and life insurance. You could save hundreds per year.

Potential savings: $50-200/month
